Monitoring public bus stops in Ilhéus

Posted on April 18, 2015October 3, 2017 by ereiser

We had the pleasure of collaborating with Nossa Ilhéus on Thursday to debut Promise Tracker in the state of Bahia.  Working with a group of student activists, media makers, professors, and members of councils on education and entrepreneurship, we developed campaigns to assess maintenance of public bus stops throughout the city.

The team decided to monitor the presence of signage, seating, covered areas and proper lighting and visited 5 bus stops in the downtown area to collect data.
